# Step 1 Results

To shortlist 5 websites from the 50 we had generated we first shortlisted the 50 websites to 11 websites 1 for each member of our team. We also evaluated each of the websites on a set [rubric](../RUBRIC.MD). That is each of us selected 1 website from out 4-5 websites according the the [rubric](../RUBRIC.MD) evaluation.

Then we came together as a team and from those 11 websites we shortlisted 5 website candidates for the first step of refinement. This was done by collectively re-evaluating those 11 websites using the same [rubric](../RUBRIC.MD).

## Observations

A few important observations we made that helped us decide which websites to shortlist are:

- We liked the websites which used emojis instead of text
- We prefered the website with sounds
- We liked the websites with consistent color themes and prefered blue over the rest (other than candidate-004)
