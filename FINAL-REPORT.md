## Final thoughts

This experiment gave us a sense how easily LLM's can become confused by seemingly obvious instructions. Sometimes, when we mentioned something in our refined prompts it would include what we mentioned but remove stuff we liked from the original website. The main takeaway we got was that LLM's are very sensitive to the prompt you give to them. They will take it quite literally but also you should specify elements you want to keep from the original boilerplate.
